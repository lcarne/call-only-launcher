Inter is used throughout the application's UI to provide excellent readability and modern aesthetics, particularly optimized for digital interfaces and senior users.

### Font Weights Used
- **Regular (400)**: Body text, descriptions
- **Medium (500)**: Labels, secondary emphasis
- **SemiBold (600)**: Section headers, primary emphasis  
- **Bold (700)**: Headings, strong emphasis
